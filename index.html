Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Oksana Lukashenko ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use the ChronoBudni mobile application.

1. Information Collection

We believe your life story belongs to you. ChronoBudni is designed to be privacy-first.

  • Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data (such as your name, address, or specific calendar entries) on our servers.
  • Usage Data: All calendar events, tasks, and journal entries entered into the App are stored locally on your device or synced via your personal Apple iCloud account. We do not have access to this data.

2. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you (e.g., for analytics or crash reporting to help us fix bugs).

3. Security

We value your trust. Since your data is stored locally or via Apple's iCloud services, it is protected by the security measures provided by Apple iOS.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
