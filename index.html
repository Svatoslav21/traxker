Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Tommy Bailey ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use the Cherta mobile application (the "Service").

1. Information Collection

We prioritize your privacy. Cherta is designed to be a personal tool.

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data to understand how the app is used and to improve stability (e.g., crash logs).
  • User Input: The financial rules and tracking data you enter are stored locally on your device or via your personal iCloud account (if enabled). We do not have access to your personal financial entries.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues.
  • To improve user experience based on anonymous feedback.

3. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. Since most data is stored locally on your device, your information is as secure as your device itself. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services (such as Apple's App Store services) that may collect information used to identify you. Please refer to Apple's privacy policy for more details.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
